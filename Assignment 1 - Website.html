Project Idea



Project Overview:

My project will be centred on the use of music informatics and machine learning to develop software that makes use of advanced algorithms to analyse the musical preferences of users in order to create individualised playlists to suit their mood. By collecting data from users listening history on streaming services such as Spotify, Apple Music and YouTube, this software will analyse elements of the music users listen to (such as tempo, chord progression, melody and genre) and use this information to automatically create highly individualised playlists that match users’ preferences and musical tastes.

Motivation for Project:

As streaming services become more and more prevalent users are faced with an almost infinite selection of music/songs to choose from. In many ways this makes the selection of what to listen to more complex for users and leaves them in a position where they have to spend considerable time searching and selecting which music they want to listen to, or instead find themselves listening to the same music over and over again.

The aim of this project will be to both simplify this process and enhance the user experience by providing users with unlimited playlists of music that are customised to their own individualised profile. By emulating Netflix’s algorithmic approach to film suggestions this software will aim to provide users with the best listening experience available and integrate this with their preferred music streaming service.

My personal motivation for this project comes both from my desire to be able to make use of a product such as this, and also from my desire to develop the skills that would prepare me for a job such as the one discussed earlier.

Project Description:

The idea of this product is to be able to provide users with individually tailored playlists and song selections/suggestions that enhance their listening experience. This product builds upon similar concepts such as ‘Create a Station’ on Apple Music and ‘Song Radio’ on Spotify but aims to improve upon these algorithms and more accurately match users’ preferences with songs.

The software would be able to work in conjunction with all the major streaming services by compiling data of what the individual listens to and also by creating playlists that can be exported to these services. In addition, it would use prompts to motivate users to rate the music that they listen to and use a series of questionnaires to build up an incredibly accurate understanding of the user’s unique musical tastes. Users would also have the option to state their current mood, or circumstances (i.e. on holiday, going through a break-up, at a party) and the algorithm would use this information to create a playlist best suited to these circumstances based on their particular profile. In addition to this there would be suggestions for new music to explore and user feedback of these suggestions would further enhance the accuracy of the profile.

The user interface would be elegant and simple to use. It would make the process of developing a unique listening profile fun and enjoyable through gamification. Essentially it would attempt to gain as much data as possible from the user but doing so in a way that does detract from the listening experience but instead adds to it. It would be a paid subscription service and it would accessible via a website, a desktop app and a smartphone app.

Required Tools and Technologies:

Starting this project, I would need to consult with various IT professionals and establish what exactly will be required (in terms of software and hardware) to create this software. I believe a large part of the success will be based on an effective use of python and machine learning to create algorithms that will be guided by music informatics research and musical theoretical analysis. It will likely be a project that requires a large degree of outside help.

Required Skills:

As this is a new field for me, I am not fully aware of the complexity of this project and the specifics of what will need to be done. I understand that it will require me to develop a much better understanding of music informatics as well as developing a solid grounding in programming. To make this project feasible I will need a great deal of external help/guidance and I will need to spend a lot of time improving my own programming skills. Having said that, realistically I believe that much of the work will need to be completed by experts and my role may focus more on music analysis and theoretical side. The software created would have to be capable of using deep learning to uncover patterns, trends and similarities in music and be able to use this information to inform song selection.

Desired Outcome:

A successful outcome for this project would see the creation of software that enhances listeners experience of music. An essential part of the project would be to create something that simplifies the process of song selection and playlist generation instead of something that adds another level of complexity. Users should find that the playlists created match their preferences and tastes. The creation of such software will be in competition with large multi-national streaming services own algorithms so the result will have to be something that is notably better and that takes little effort on the part of the user. It would be a very ambitious project to undertake but I believe it would be a product that would have mass-market appeal.
